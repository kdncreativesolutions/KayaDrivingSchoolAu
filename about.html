Our History

Gaurang Patel

Gaurang Patel is a professional driving instructor and the founder of KAYA Driving School , established in Canberra, ACT with a strong commitment to road safety and driver confidence.

With years of experience teaching learners across the ACT, Gaurang Patel has helped hundreds of students become safe and confident drivers through patient, student-focused lessons tailored to real-world driving conditions and ACT licensing requirements. His mission is not only to help learners pass their driving test, but also to prepare them for a lifetime of safe driving.

  • Professional and experienced driving instructor
  • SDERA certified and ACT-compliant training
  • Focused on safe and defensive driving skills
  • Patient, supportive, and beginner-friendly approach
  • Trusted by learners across Canberra, ACT

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Safety Above All

Every decision we make is guided by a commitment to road safety for our students, instructors, and the wider Canberra community.

Student First

We tailor every lesson to the individual learner's skills, anxiety levels, and goals. There is no one-size-fits-all approach here.

Excellence Driven

We continuously update our training methods and invest in our fleet to deliver a world-class driving education experience.

Community Focused

We are proud members of the Canberra community, creating safer roads in our city by producing responsible, skilled drivers.

Continuous Learning

Our instructors undergo regular professional development to stay updated with the latest driving standards and techniques.

Positive Experience

Learning to drive should be enjoyable. We create a calm, positive, and encouraging environment in every single lesson.

Driving School
