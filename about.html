Our History

Founded on a Passion for Road Safety

Kaya Driving School was established in Canberra, ACT with a simple but powerful vision — to create a driving school where every student feels supported, confident, and equipped with real-world driving skills.

Over a decade later, we've helped more than 2,000 Canberra residents earn their licences. Our structured curriculum, patient instructors, and student-first approach set us apart from every other driving school in the ACT.

  • Established in Canberra, ACT — proudly local
  • SDERA certified and industry-accredited instructors
  • Modern fleet of 5-Star ANCAP safety-rated vehicles
  • RMS-compliant lesson program tailored to ACT licensing

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Safety Above All

Every decision we make is guided by a commitment to road safety — for our students, instructors, and the wider Canberra community.

Student First

We tailor every lesson to the individual learner's skills, anxiety levels, and goals. There is no one-size-fits-all approach here.

Excellence Driven

We continuously update our training methods and invest in our fleet to deliver a world-class driving education experience.

Community Focused

We are proud members of the Canberra community, creating safer roads in our city by producing responsible, skilled drivers.

Continuous Learning

Our instructors undergo regular professional development to stay updated with the latest driving standards and techniques.

Positive Experience

Learning to drive should be enjoyable. We create a calm, positive, and encouraging environment in every single lesson.
